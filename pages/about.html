About SingFinTech

A Singapore-based financial technology company developing AI-driven investment systems and advanced trading intelligence. Through Cep Portfoy, we empower every investor with tools that were once reserved for institutions.

Its flagship brand, Cep Portfoy, represents the next generation of digital portfolio management — where artificial intelligence, data analytics, and behavioral economics converge to reshape global investing.

Bridging Human Insight with Machine Precision

Our Mission: To make intelligent investment accessible to everyone by integrating deep learning algorithms with financial theory, helping investors make smarter, faster, and more confident decisions. Cep Portfoy automates complex market analyses and provides tailored insights for individuals and institutions alike.

Our Vision: By 2030, we aim to establish SingFinTech as a global standard for AI-powered investing — creating a borderless ecosystem connecting traders, analysts, and investors through transparent and secure digital platforms. The future of finance belongs to those who innovate — and innovation is in our DNA.

We've become a driving force in financial innovation across Asia, Europe, and the Middle East, with offices in Singapore, Istanbul, and London.

Our Philosophy:Investment is both science and art. SingFinTech's purpose is to unite analytical rigor with human creativity. We believe that sustainable success is achieved when technology enhances human potential, not replaces it.

Through Cep Portfoy, we offer every investor the chance to act with institutional precision and individual freedom. Our technology doesn't make decisions for you — it empowers you to make better decisions yourself.
